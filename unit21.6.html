1.6 Describing access issues that need taking into account - describe issues that people with disabilities may face when using webpages and how we can overcome them

A good example for this would be a person with bad sight, if there is an image that their vision can't comprehend you can title the picture so it can be read with a speech reader. For someone who is deaf they could be looking up a news report that has a video and they would not be able to understand it unless they can lip read, or there is subtitles for them to read. OR it could have on screen signers like some news channels use.
