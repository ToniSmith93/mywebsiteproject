1.5 Describing copyright and other constraints affecting websites.

Copyright laws effect websites because it restricts what you are able to post on your pages. For example if you use someone elses content on your website without permission then they can sue you. The best way to do this is to get ask for permission before using whatever it is, if they say no then you're unable to use it and will have to find an alternative and you would have to go through the same process for the other content that you want to use. So the best way to avoid this is to use your own content.
