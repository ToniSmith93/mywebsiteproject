1.7: Describe which are the appropriate filetypes for websites


Appropriate filetypes for websites include:
.CSS .HTML and JavaScript. These are used in all websites because these are what give websites their look and functionality.
For example, a website that is al HTML will look very uninviting because the styling of the webpage is done in CSS.
JavaScript is used for more complex things in webpages, such as animating a live clock that updates by the second, or by the minute, it all depends on how you programme it.
