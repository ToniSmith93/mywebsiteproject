1.4: Describe the risks that might impact digital projects. - What causes digital projects to fail? Why?

Risks on projects come from a lot of places:
A lack of funding or funding being reallocated for higher priority projects.
Too much to do at once.
Not enough people to do each job so they have to do several jobs at once which can severely slow down production time.
This is why proper planning is important so you can pre-assign requirements such as one job per person, not something like three jobs for one person, two for someone else and four for another person, which i touched on a bit in 1.2.

As an example, a film company want to make a movie based on a game series, they would first need to get the correct licences and permissions which can sometimes take a long time to confirm before they can actually start the project.
